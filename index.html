About

After graduating in 2015 with a bachelor's degree in geography from the University of British Columbia, I settled in Seattle and began working at a publishing company. I've since found my passion—web development—and am continually building upon my skill set on a daily basis.

Despite being relatively new to coding, I believe my background will serve me well in my professional career. Early in my college education, I had planned to pursue a career as an illustrator/oil painter. Although I instead decided to major in something more academic and practical, I retained my eye for what works and what doesn't in design. I also ran a personal finance blog for half a year during a period of career path exploration. My experience as a proofreader and web content writer has honed my ability to communicate clearly and effectively.

During my spare time I also enjoy listening to science- and philosophy-related podcasts and working on my French.
